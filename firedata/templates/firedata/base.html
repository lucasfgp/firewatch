Glossary


Burning Event

A recorded occurrence of fire activity within a conservation unit, which may include wildfires, controlled burns, firebreak maintenance, or other fire-related interventions aimed at management, prevention, or response.


Conservation Units

Conservation Units (CUs) are natural areas protected by brazilian law. These areas possess unique characteristics related to the local fauna and flora.


Management Unit

An administrative division responsible for overseeing the protection, conservation, and sustainable use of natural areas within a specific geographical boundary, including the coordination of activities in one or more conservation units.
